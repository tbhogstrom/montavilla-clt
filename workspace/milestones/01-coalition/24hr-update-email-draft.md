Hey {{firstName}} —

You signed the Montavilla Community Land Trust petition yesterday. You were one of seven people who did. I want to be straight with you about where things stand after 24 hours.

**What happened yesterday:**

We launched the campaign at the Montavilla Farmers Market on Sunday and posted to Reddit. The Reddit thread got 94 upvotes and 78 comments before it was taken down — and the feedback was honest. Some people love the idea. A lot of people think the $5M asking price is absurd. Many said the lot should be housing. Others suggested the market could move to a cheaper location.

Here's the thing: they're not wrong to ask those questions. And we're listening.

**What we're actually building:**

This isn't about one predetermined plan. It's about whether the Montavilla neighborhood gets a voice in what happens to the lot at 7700 SE Stark — or whether that decision gets made by a real estate investor in Michigan and whatever developer writes the biggest check.

A community land trust means the community decides. Maybe that's a year-round market commons. Maybe it's affordable housing with market space. Maybe it's something none of us have thought of yet. The point is: it should be Montavilla's call.

**Where we need help this week:**

Right now we are 7 people and $1,175 in pledges. That's a start, not a movement. Here's what would make a real difference:

1. **Print 10 flyers and hand them to people you know** — neighbors, the person at the coffee shop, your barber. The flyer is one page, designed to print at home: [montavillalandtrust.org/flyer](https://www.montavillalandtrust.org/flyer)

2. **Talk to 3 people about it this week** — not a sales pitch, just: "Did you know the farmers market lot is for sale? Some neighbors are trying to organize around it." See what they think. Their questions are as valuable as their support.

3. **Come to the Farmers Market next Sunday (April 19)** — We need people handing out flyers and having conversations. Even one hour matters. If you can come, reply to this email and tell me what time works.

4. **Share the website with one person who might care** — [montavillalandtrust.org](https://www.montavillalandtrust.org)

**What I'm working on this week:**

- Reaching out to the Montavilla Farmers Market organization and the Montavilla Neighborhood Association. We need them at the table.
- Getting a real assessment of what the property is actually worth (multiple people say $5M is way above market).
- Looking into the property owner and listing situation.
- Planning our first community meeting — a real one, in person, where neighbors can ask hard questions and shape what this becomes.

**One more thing:**

You signed up on day one. That makes you a founding member, not a follower. If you have skills we need — legal, finance, real estate, design, community organizing, or just strong opinions about Montavilla — reply to this email. I want to hear from you.

Seven people isn't a lot. But every coalition starts somewhere.
