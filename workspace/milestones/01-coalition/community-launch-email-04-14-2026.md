Hey —

We just launched the community forum at **montavillalandtrust.org/community** — and I want to explain why it matters more than it might look.

**What it is:**

Eight conversations are already open. What does the market mean to you. What should live in the community hub. What a year-round market actually looks like. Who else should be at the table. Hard questions about the CLT model — and the history of this corner of Montavilla that most people don't know.

These aren't rhetorical. I genuinely don't know the answers. The point of this forum is to find them together, in public, where the conversation becomes the record of what this neighborhood actually wants.

**Why this matters right now:**

Here's where we are: 7 signatures. $1,175 pledged. That's day one.

To generate the community wave we need — to move toward real consensus, to give funders and the city something to point to, to sit across a table from a seller and say *this neighborhood is organized* — we need to keep doubling. Not forever. Just consistently.

7 becomes 14 becomes 28 becomes 56. That's not a lot of people, but it's a visible, growing, documented signal. The forum is part of that signal. A thread with 40 replies from real Montavilla neighbors is evidence. A thread with zero replies is just a website.

**What I'm asking:**

1. **Go to montavillalandtrust.org/community** and read what's there.
2. **Reply to one conversation.** Even two sentences. Tell us what the market means to you, or what you'd want in the community hub, or what you're skeptical about.
3. **Send this email to one person** who should be part of this conversation and isn't yet.

That's it.

The forum doesn't require an account. You don't need to introduce yourself. Just say something real.

**One more ask:**

If you haven't pledged yet — do it today. montavillalandtrust.org/calculator. Every dollar is public. Every dollar is evidence. We need the pledge number to double the same way we need the signature number to double. 

We're building something worth building. But only if enough people show up.

See you in the forum.
