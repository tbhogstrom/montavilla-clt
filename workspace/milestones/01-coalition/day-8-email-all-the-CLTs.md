## Email Body

Hi —

My name is Tyler Falcon. I founded Montavilla Community Land Trust, in Portland, Oregon, eight days ago.

Since then I've put together an inventory of every CLT I could find in the United States. Catalogued them. Organized them by state. Tracked down your websites. Found the emails the hard way. You know — for science. Mostly for community.

**[montavillalandtrust.org/clt-inventory](https://www.montavillalandtrust.org/clt-inventory)** — 329 CLTs, 49 states, as of today. If your entry is wrong, reply and I'll fix it today.

**Why I built it:**

After operating for 19 years, the Montavilla Farmers Market lot — 7700 SE Stark — went up for sale at $5M. I decided to do something about it. Eight days later, I've read every page I could find on CLTs, started running campaigns, and started asking the harder question underneath all of it: *has anyone actually done this before?*

Turns out: yes. Hundreds of you. In 48 states. It's the reason I'm writing.

**Why I'm writing you:**

Montavilla CLT sits at the intersection of commercial / agricultural community space centered on a farmers market. Most CLTs are housing. A smaller number hold ag land. Fewer still do commercial. I may have found some peers, but I haven't met them yet.

So I'm reaching out to all of you, at once, because I don't know which of you has already solved the problem I'm about to trip over. If any of the following describes your CLT, I would be grateful for 10 minutes of your email:

1. **You started around a single threatened property.** What did the first 90 days actually look like? What do you wish someone had told you?
2. **You hold commercial or agricultural land.** How did you structure the ground lease? How did you think about vendor and tenant governance alongside resident governance?
3. **You raised pre-acquisition capital before you had a legal entity.** How?
4. **You know of a CLT in Oregon, Washington, or Northern California** that might want to talk with a new one. An intro would be a gift.

I am not asking for money. I am not pitching anything. I am a new neighbor-founder with an open notebook and a closing window.

**What we've built in 8 days, in case any of it is useful:**

- A petition and pledge tool — small numbers, but real and growing.
- A plain-language explainer of what a CLT is, aimed at people who have never heard the term: **[montavillalandtrust.org/what-is-a-clt](https://www.montavillalandtrust.org/what-is-a-clt)**
- A research synthesis of what we learned from your movement: **[montavillalandtrust.org/research](https://www.montavillalandtrust.org/research)**
- The inventory above.

**One ask if you have time for nothing else:**

If your CLT has a page, a template, a war story, or a "here's-what-we-wish-we-knew" document — forward it. Even a two-sentence reply that says "read this, ignore that" is worth its weight.

Thanks for the work you've done. A lot of what you've built is the reason what we're trying to build is thinkable at all.
