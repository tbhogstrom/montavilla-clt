## ⚠️ STRATEGIC DISCUSSION NEEDED: Does the Plaza Finding Change Our Acquisition Strategy?

The Montavilla Plaza at 79th & Stark already hosts a market. If it can be expanded to handle the full Sunday market, the $5M (or even $2.5M) lot acquisition may be unnecessary for the market's survival. This would mean:

1. The "save the market" mission could be achieved via plaza expansion (cheap, fast, community-supported)
2. The CLT's focus could shift to broader community land stewardship (the lot, alternative sites, or both)
3. The PCEF planning grant could fund the community engagement process to determine next steps
4. We'd enter any property negotiation from a position of strength ("we have alternatives") rather than desperation

**This needs a decision from Statton and the steering group.** The plaza finding doesn't eliminate the CLT — it strengthens it by giving us options. But it reframes the urgency from "we must buy this lot or lose the market" to "we have time to do this right."
