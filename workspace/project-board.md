## Critical Path & Risks

### Credibility Gap (CRITICAL — raised by community)
No endorsement from Montavilla Farmers Market or MNA. AI content flagged. No legal entity. The community told us directly: prove you're real before asking for money.

### Time Risk (MEDIUM — downgraded)
Property listed at $5M but expert consensus says no developer will pay that. Michigan-based absentee owner with inflated ask. We likely have months, not weeks. Still monitor weekly.

### Framing Risk (HIGH — new)
Current website says "SAVE THIS LOT" and presents one predetermined outcome. This contradicts the community-first, outcome-open strategy. Website reframe is urgent.

### Legal Structure Gap (MEDIUM)
We are not yet a legal entity. Cannot receive tax-deductible donations, apply for grants, or sign contracts until we incorporate. Fiscal sponsorship could bridge.

### Proud Ground Relationship (MEDIUM)
Referenced throughout the prospectus but not formalized. Need to clarify role.
